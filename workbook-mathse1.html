1.1.7 (積和公式).

cos(x)cos(y) = (cos(x + y) + cos(x − y))

1.1.8 (負角公式).

cos(−x) = cos(x)
sin(−x) = −sin(x)
tan(−x) = −tan(x)

1.1.9 (余角公式).

cos = cos(x)
sin = sin(x)
tan = cot(x)

1.1.10 (補角公式).

cos(π − x) = −cos(x)
sin(π − x) = sin(x)
tan(π − x) = −tan(x)
